My favourite things to do in The Malverns

What are The Malverns?

The Malvern Hills, or The Malverns, are a beautiful range of hills in the west of England's midlands county of Worcestershire. I moved here with my husband from London six years ago to start a family, and now have two little girls.

What do the hills offer?

There are lots of wonderful things to do in The Malverns, but these are what I love best:
